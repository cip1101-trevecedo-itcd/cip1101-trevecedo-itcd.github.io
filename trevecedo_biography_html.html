      Childhood Years

   "looking back through the looking glasss"

Year (2002) where I was born in Cabanatuan city, Nueva Ecija. My parents were as happy as they can be since their second child is a boy.(1) Year (2003) I learned to walk as much as I can hug my family tightly, that it makes them happy every time I do.(2) Year (2005) Oh blissful memories I remember, playing, jumping, crying and loving such are the feelings that makes a house, home.(3) Year (2008) My first day of school!!, I was so happy that I get to meet other children that are the same age as me.(4) I got new friends, we talk about crazy stuff like we thought that rain comes from angels that are peeing through the sky, disgusting but those are merely the innocent imagination of youth.(5)


Year (2008) oh caught you off guard, time didn’t really move for me back then, I only remember watching T.V with my sister when my father was acting strange saying things I couldn’t remember, next thing happened I found myself in a broken family.(6) My mother crying like she didn’t do anything like it was my father’s fault, crazy adults huh.(7) But to be honest I didn’t really hate them, if they aren’t happy being together then just go separate ways, it’s not like it will affect their children right.(8) I just wish it didn’t affect their children.(9)


Year (2009) I’m now living with my grandma, sister, uncle and my mother.(10) We live in a small house in a subdivision where good things happen at least in my memories.(11) Year (2009) I started second grade middle school, I am studying in a private school, kids there are rich as hell, while I don’t really envy them, I just wish that I could have what they have someday.(12) Year (2010) I love this year, this was the year I met my childhood crush her name is angel, little did she know well, I think she really is an angel; she is also my first close friend.(13) I met her when I was looking around then there was this girl, she’s so energetic yet her face remains as beautiful as her movements, she sat 2 chairs aways from me, but hey those aren’t going to stop me.(14) Recess time I had a chance to talk to her but you know I couldn't find the words; I couldn't think of what to say and all that I can do is stop and think about the day.(15)


Year (2011) This time I had a squad if that’s what they call it, but it’s a circle of friends nothing really happened much in this year that is worth mentioning.(16) Year (2012) Grade five, I couldn’t stop thinking about angel even after all those years she’s still her (most beautiful person in his eyes), This time something happened between us that I couldn’t forget, she taught me how to ride a bike, we’re friends so it’s pretty normal, but to me if memories can be treasured then I’ll treasure this as long as I’m breathing.(17)


After all the riding we have done, well we’re tired sat somewhere where we can see the ground below us.(18) Breathing hard she spoke something, she tells me that we’re like siblings, she wished that we don’t go separate ways after graduating grade school, well I wish that too, I even wish for her to only look at me and me alone but things does not go the way you like and that’s sad.(19) Year (2013) We graduated, my grandma is with me, I’m standing in front like everybody else, those kids are crying while me I don’t really feel like crying.(20) But deep inside I was the saddest person out there.(21) I didn’t really have the chance to meet her one last time I just thought that (When all we used to dream about was meeting after school, and we would go on home and start right back again) but really, I was just ashamed that I broke our promise back then that we will still be together after graduating.(22)

      Teenage Years 

     "Fluorescent Adolescent"

Year (2014), Today I’m 12 years old about to go 13 years old, I’m now studying at manila with my sister and father.(1) My grandma was very sad that we have to live not around her anymore, I was sad as well.(2) And all the things that are unsaid should be left unsaid as I move on with my life, cleared eye.(3) Year (2015) 2nd year of high school, I started playing as the school’s chess participant didn’t really enjoy that much, I just like playing chess but I don’t have any dreams about being a professional chess player.(4)


Year (2016) 3rd year of high school, this time I get to feel the feeling of falling in love again, her name is Ericka she sat beside me and she is a transfer student.(5) In the four-sided room I could feel the temperature getting lower and lower while she is checking my sketch pad, telling things that I couldn’t clearly hear, it’s just that she looks so clear and beautiful, I was clearly thinking of nothing.(6)


Days goes by like the wind, fast yet you feel the breeze once in a while, it makes your skin tingle and it’s annoying.(7) During this year (2016) I joined a different club, it was math club I personally don’t like math but I still ended up there due to my curiosity(8). I was accompanied by my friends that is also curious about the club, while not knowing that the professor that are waiting for us there is known to be a chick strict as hell.(9) We arrived at the club, sat at the nearby chair and sit beside each other that was our plan, to lessen the terror.(10)


Eventually the professor arrived and surprisingly we have seen another side of her.(11) She was actually kind of caring motherly like figure outside of teaching and lecturing.(12) That time it teaches me that not everything you thought is what they are actually like sometimes it’s just the other side of the coin.(13) Year (2017) 10th grade this year felt like it was rushed or maybe because most of the time I was busy as hell.(14) All of my classmates ran through here and there, like there is a zombie apocalypse, honestly you can’t talk to anybody that time, everybody is just busy well I am too so I’ll cut this short.(15)


After some time, we graduated but it’s not that grand or emotional since all of us will stay in the school and not enroll to other school which makes me kind of happy.(16) After some time, we have the chance to take it easy once in a while, it was the day for our prom night.(17) I was very excited to look cool and neat once in a while since I’m not really that kind of guy that looks cool in school uniforms.(18)


Honestly the thing that I’m most excited is to see her in such a beautiful woven dress that makes it look like it was made for her, eyes shining like it was made of diamond or maybe it is, well just kidding.(19) After that night me and my friends hit the bar well we look much older for our age that’s why we get a free pass, but it wasn’t that kind of bar it’s just a simple bar.(20) That night I was thinking, in this starry night my teenage years would probably come into a pass that I need to focus in school more and much more, but if pretending to know was childish and pretending not to know was adult, then I thought that I was still probably just a child.(21)

      College Years 

 "I think I figure out what was so different now compared to back then... It was myself"

Year (2021) first year of college, it was really hard to choose which college I should choose, there was a lot of things I should consider.(1) Those things are how much the school would cost, well even if I like this school there was no way I could get in considering the amount of money needed for me to study there, so pricey schools are a no.(2) Next is how far the university is, it should only be as far as one to two jeepney rides, so it will not be such a hassle going to school.(3) Lastly is the quality of teaching, universities have different quality of teaching depending on the course.(4)


So, considering these 3 factors I ended up applying for "University of the East" also I’m not that knowledgeable to be able to apply for schools like UP, I already tried though but didn’t get the cut.(5) Year (2021) august my first day of college is about to start, I’m really nervous since I’m not really attractive and it’s annoying to open my camera well it’s not like they care to whatever I look but still.(6) So, then it started I met a number of good friends they really are good to have by my side not only they help me at academic but also helps me to get by this new environment I have settled myself in.(7)


I started the year very normal, like a normal college student I have to juggle my time between school and personal life more deliberate so that I won’t diverge to a stressful schedule.(8) These times I don’t really have much time of falling in love like I do back then since all of them does not really end well, but I can’t lie to myself that Ericka is still part of my mind but she is not my priority anymore, I have to carry on and focus on my life instead (well that’s what I think).(9) Year (2021) Midyear I’m starting to think that being an IT is really one of the best things that have happened to my life well that’s what I thought.(10) School year was about to end and I think that I got very good grades and eventually I was able to be part of the scholars.(11) Well, I was part of the scholars not because I was so intelligent well, I’m average but also, it’s because some of my friends help me as well.(12)


Year (2021) It’s the beginning of a new chapter which is my 2nd year college well it’s still not done yet but there are a couple of things that already happened.(13) For instance I was able to dodge professors that I don’t like well it’s not that they are bad professors, but they are not good for my mental health so as much as possible I try to dodge them.(14) Still the same year (2021) things are a bit better we started second semester as normal as any semester, but the usual me seems to be a bit gone.(15) Since my grandmother passed away, accomplishments in school doesn’t feel the same way because my grandma wasn’t there to congratulate me and be happy since back then I wasn’t really an achiever and to her every achievement I make is a core memory and I love that.(16)


Nights seems a little bit longer maybe a little bit cold as well, I think it’s 3am in the morning I rode my bike packing stuff that I think I might need, In the road or in the bed either way it does not change the fact that I keep thinking of my grandma I just love her so much that a part of me has to go.(17) Knees tired and my face is as red as an apple, I rest beside a tree it’s shadow seems to be talking to me telling me things that I already know, in fact that day I thought that my old life was probably the part that left me.(18)


Mid (2021) oh the cold days, pandemic is still at its peak, its starting to feel like it’s already a part of the world ha what am I thinking of course eventually it would have an end just like everything else.(19) We studied a new programming language it’s called java well we studied it last year as well but today we learned quite a couple of new things.(20) Time pass by and today we are at the end of our first semester, it was very tiring to the point that most of us probably have a point in which they started having breakdowns, oh you ask me? Well fortunately I didn’t have any breakdowns of some sort.(21) To summarize we did a lot of things we made a game, software’s and a lot of interesting things.(22) Fast forward, end of the year it was about to turn 2022 maybe 5 minutes before 2022 I don’t really care much all I care is that I’m about to leave the feelings that must be left behind this year, huh what am I thinking changing of years doesn’t change the fact that all that happened to me did affect me in some sort of way, year changing doesn’t really means to start a new, to me what it meant is too look back all the things that has happened that makes me who am I today.(23)
